What is LEAGUE OF LEGENDS WILD RIFT

League of Legends: Wild Rift (abbreviated LoL: WR or simply Wild Rift) is a multiplayer online battle arena mobile game developed and published by Riot Games for Android and iOS. The free-to-play game is a modified version of the PC game League of Legends.

AESPA

Aespa (Korean: 에스파; RR: eseupa; MR: esŭp'a, commonly stylized in all lowercase or æspa) is a South Korean girl group formed by SM Entertainment. The group consists of four members: Karina, Giselle, Winter and Ningning. They debuted on November 17, 2020, with the single, "Black Mamba".

Webtoon Artist

Webtoon artists often post their comics on many sites including social media. In the past few years, they have surged in popularity among the anime/comics fan communities. Webtoons are created by independent artists and if people like their work, they support them through Patreon.
